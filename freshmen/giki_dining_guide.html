A Guide to Dining at GIKI

Everything you need to know about food on campus.

Dining at GIKI: Mess & More

GIKI has a central mess system that caters to all students. Additionally, there are several other tuck shops and cafes on campus for when you want a change of pace.

Student Mess Halls

There are designated mess halls for each hostel. The food is served at specific times, and the menu is a mix of Pakistani cuisines, rotating on a weekly basis.

  • Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Lunch: 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM
  • Dinner: 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Tip: You can opt-out of the mess on a daily basis (before 2pm) if you prefer, but it's generally the a convenient option.

Other Eateries on Campus

For snacks, late-night cravings, or just a different taste, you can explore:

  • Hot and Spicy: Located at tuc. By far the best fast food you can find in GIKI.
  • Ayan and Raju for Desi: Also located at tuc. These restaurants, although have a wide variety of pakistani cuisine and fast food, they lack the feel (the hygiene and taste)
  • GIKI Cafe: Owned and run by GIKI admin itself. Its located under the auditorium (yes that circular building you see almost everywhere) Its somewhat similar to mess when it comes to taste and hygiene. Its good for tea and some light snacks like nuggets, samsosas or rolls. You can also come for dinner here, its cheaper than mess, so if you wanna save, Cafe is the cheapest option ya got :)
