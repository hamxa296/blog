About GIKI Chronicles

The authentic voice of student life in the Valley.

Our Mission

GIKI Chronicles is a student-run platform dedicated to capturing the diverse stories, groundbreaking projects, and unique experiences that define life at the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute. We aim to create a space where every student can share their voice, from late-night project struggles to the triumphs of campus events. Our mission is to build a living archive of our collective journey, fostering a stronger, more connected community through the power of storytelling.
